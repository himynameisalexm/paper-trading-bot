A trade MUST have a clear directional RSI edge at entry. Neutral RSI without a named catalyst is NOT a valid entry:

- **RSI < 35**: Valid entry zone for oversold bounce plays (Priority 3). RSI < 30 = high priority.
- **RSI 35–55**: NEUTRAL zone. Only valid entry with an extraordinary named catalyst (tier-1 analyst upgrade, earnings beat, major sector news). Without catalyst → confidence cannot reach 7.0.
- **RSI 55–75**: Valid for momentum plays (Priority 2, 5) IF there is a specific catalyst and price is above MA50. RSI 70-75 = elevated but tradeable with catalyst.

**Why this matters:** Entering at neutral RSI (40-65) with moderate volume produces flat, directionless trades that reach neither the +5% target nor the -2.5% stop. The 3-day hold only pays off when there is a genuine directional catalyst at entry.
